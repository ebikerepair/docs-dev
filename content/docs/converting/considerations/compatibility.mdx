  <Step>
    ### Can I use the battery and display with the new motor?

    <Callout type="warning">If you switch your motor, it is **always** necessary to also switch battery and display.</Callout>

    This is due to the manufacturers' proprietary battery protocols and the requirements of the new motor. For example, it may only accept a battery with a voltage of 48V, while your old battery only delivers 36V. Or the motor might require more power than the current battery could deliver anyway.
